Overview

Our city park offers open lawns, shaded groves, and a small lake. It’s a calm place to run, read, or picnic with friends.

Trails

Lakeside Loop

1.8 km — flat gravel path with views of ducks and reeds.

Oak Ridge

2.5 km — gentle climb under tall oaks; good shade at noon.

Meadow Walk

1.2 km — open grass, wildflowers in late spring.

Wildlife

  • Common: squirrels, ducks, herons
  • Seasonal: monarch butterflies (late Aug–Sept)
  • Please observe from a distance and don’t feed animals.

Visitor Tips

  • Best hours: early morning or late afternoon
  • Bring water and sunscreen on warm days
  • Leashed dogs are welcome on all trails

Quick Info

  • Open: 6am – 9pm
  • Bathrooms: Near the lake
  • Picnic Areas: North meadow
